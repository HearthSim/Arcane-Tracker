Arcane Tracker privacy policy

 

Arcane Tracker is a Hearthstone tracker for Android. By default, all game data is recorded on your phone and is only accessible by you. You can enable thrid-parties services to save this data in the cloud and/or share it on hsreplay.net.

For any question, feel free to contact hello@arcanetracker.com.

Hearthstone logs

Arcane Tracker reads the Hearthstone logs to analyze the games you play. These files contain your battle.net tag as well as the details of the cards played during a game. This information is used to build the overlay as well as your win/loss count and game history. The game history is stored on your phone and protected by Android security model.

trackobot.com

Trackobot.com is an optional third party service that allows you to store your game history in the cloud so you can access in from anywhere as well as see statistics about your games. If you opted-in, you can opt-out any time by going to settins -> Track-o-bot -> unlink your account.

hsreplay.net

hsreplay.net is an optional third party service that allows you to store your games so you can replay or share them. If you opted-in, you can opt-out any time by going to settins -> hsreplay.net and unchecking the checkbox.

Firebase analytics

Arcane Tracker uses Firebase Analytics to measure its audience. All data is anonymous and contains no personal information.
